GDK Documentation Archive

Welcome to the unofficial official GDK documentation archive. This archive may be full of broken links, content errors, etc., but it’s simply here to be a backup just in case Google ever gets rid of the official GDK documentation. If you do happen to find a broken link, content error, or something else, please submit an issue!
